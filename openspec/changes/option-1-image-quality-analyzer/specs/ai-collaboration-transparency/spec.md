## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AI collaboration disclosure
The submission MUST disclose which parts were assisted by AI and which parts were modified, verified, or decided by the human author.

#### Scenario: Reviewer reads AI collaboration notes
- **WHEN** a reviewer opens the AI collaboration document
- **THEN** the document separates AI contributions from human verification and final decisions

### Requirement: Question iteration record
The submission SHALL include a concise record of how the author decomposed and iterated questions while using AI.

#### Scenario: Reviewer checks questioning process
- **WHEN** a reviewer opens the AI collaboration document
- **THEN** the document shows multiple question iterations from broad requirement analysis to specific implementation or validation decisions

### Requirement: AI error or limitation record
The submission MUST include at least one AI suggestion, assumption, or output that was rejected or corrected after human judgment.

#### Scenario: Reviewer checks critical thinking evidence
- **WHEN** a reviewer opens the AI collaboration document
- **THEN** the document describes the AI output, why it was insufficient or wrong, how it was verified, and what decision replaced it

### Requirement: Rubric alignment
The submission SHALL explicitly map evidence to the contest rubric categories: passion, critical thinking, questioning ability, integration ability, and learning ability.

#### Scenario: Reviewer checks scoring alignment
- **WHEN** a reviewer opens the scoring alignment document
- **THEN** the document lists concrete evidence for each rubric category
